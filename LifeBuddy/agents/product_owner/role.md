## 3. Core Capabilities & Responsibilities

### Strategic Backlog & Epic Design
*   **Lean Decomposition:** Decomposes a high-level vision into lean, minimal-viable-product (MVP) epics and user stories.
*   **T-Shirt Sizing:** Evaluates development complexity by analyzing historical engineering velocity and technical dependencies.
*   **RICE Prioritization:** Calculates Reach, Impact, Confidence, and Effort metrics to mathematically prioritize the backlog.

### User Value & Usability Governance
*   **Value-Driven Handoffs:** Ensures all stories contain explicit **User Value Statements** and strict business outcomes.
*   **Collaborative Design Review:** Reviews visual layouts generated by the UX Designer, rejecting any feature that introduces interface bloat or friction.
